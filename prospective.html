For Prospective Graduate Students

Am I taking students?

Thanks for your interest! I'll be recruiting a small number of graduate students this admissions cycle, to start working together in Fall 2023. I'm particularly interested in working on AI-assisted programming, language grounding, interactive systems, and pragmatics.

How to apply?

The best way to work with me (and many great folks) is to apply to LTI's PhD and/or MLT programs. The application deadline for this year is December 12th, 2022 3pm EST. There's more information here.

Should you email me?

I'd love to be able to reply to every email I get about admissions, but I just don't have the capacity, unfortunately.

It's definitely not necessary to email me, and probably won't affect your chances of admission. If you do email, though, I encourage you to describe the specific overlap in our interests in your email, and I encourage you to email after you've applied. (I can't speak for other faculty, but I'm personally fine with getting these types of tailored emails.) Unfortunately, I can't generally respond emails about admissions, sorry! But we all do really appreciate your interest and time applying.

Yonatan Bisk has a page with more context about the application process.
